Thai restuarant

There is no better place to be in Leeds if you are looking for a buzzing Bangkok Markert atmsphere in Yorkshire than your Leeds Zaap!As soon as you step into Zaap you will feel like you have been transported to Thailand minus the 11 hour cabin time and a slight jetlag. Inside, they serve freshly made streetfood that taste just like what you would get from the streets of Bangkok. The quirky interior desighn is different and enough to put you ina good mood and ready to start your night or day out with a bang. I would strongly recommend gettting a cocktail or two from the bar. They mix up things you wouldnt think to out together and make up the most delicious drinks and all this at a good price!
